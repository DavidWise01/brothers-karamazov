# THE BROTHERS KARAMAZOV · BKZ · UD0

Fyodor Dostoevsky's 1880 final novel, catalogued into **UD0** as a book-world — a parricide
wrapped around the deepest argument in fiction between faith and atheism.

Standing template adapted for the novel: The Arc · The Book · The Ideas · **The Question**
(the philosophical/theological deep-dive — *if God is dead, is everything permitted?*) ·
**The Verdict** (does the argument hold — honest) · The Message. 16 emergents by
emergence-nature (earth & flesh / the ideas / faith & active love / the fever & passion),
full `.dlw` each. Public domain; literary commentary under the DLW standard.

> He built the case against God so well it has never been refuted — and answered it not with
> an argument but with active love, and "Hurrah for Karamazov!"
